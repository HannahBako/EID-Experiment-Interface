Instructions

Thank you for participating in our study. In this session, you will be working with the experimenter to come up with visualization designs for a given dataset. In the following pages, you will be asked to:

  1. Read and understand a design task provided to you. For example, you may be asked:
    Given the cars dataset, create a visualization that shows how the mileage per gallon of cars has changed over the year
  2. You will spend 5 minutes to brainstorm and sketch out visualization design ideas
  3. You will be provided with an interface to find relevant visualization examples as you think through your designs
  4. You will be asked to upload your design sketches and select two designs you would like to implement with the experimenter
